# G-FORCE

G-FORCE is a 2D puzzle-platformer where the core mechanic revolves around manipulating gravity. The player cannot jump, instead, they invert gravity to control player's y axis position.

# How to Play

## Goal: Reach the portal at the end of the level without touching hazards like spikes.

## Controls:
*   A / D - Move Left / Right
*   W - Flip gravity up 
*   S - Flip gravity down

## Tips:
*   You cannot jump! Use W and S to flip the gravity.
*   You can only flip gravity when touching a solid floor or ceiling.
